Hlangana no-Kai — enye indlela ye-OpenClaw ephaketheni lakho. Uhlelo lokusebenza lwe-AI lokukhuluma olusheshayo, oluvulekile, olusekelwa amamodeli afana ne-Llama, Gemini, GPT, kanye ne-DeepSeek. Xoxa ngokukhululeka ukuze ucabange, ubhale, ufingqe, ufunde, noma uxoxe nje — konke ngesixhumanisi esilula esilayisha ngomzuzwana.

Izinsizakalo ze-AI Ezisekelwayo
• Ollama / LM Studio: Xhuma amamodeli e-AI aziphathele wena endaweni (ahambisana ne-OpenAI)
• OpenAI: Finyelela amamodeli e-GPT ezingxoxo ezinamandla
• Gemini: I-AI ephezulu ye-Google enesekelo lokufaka amafayela elingenakiwe
• DeepSeek: Amamodeli okucabanga asebenza kahle kakhulu
• xAI: Amamodeli e-Grok abukhali nezimpendulo ezibonisa ukuqonda
• Mistral AI: Amamodeli abalulekile ezilimi eziningi
• Groq: Ukucabanga kwe-AI okusheshayo kakhulu kwezimpendulo ezisheshayo
• OpenRouter: Finyelela amakhulu amamodeli nge-API eyodwa
• NVIDIA: Sebenzisa amamodeli asingathwe yi-NVIDIA
• Noma iyiphi i-API ehambisana ne-OpenAI: Letha indawo yakho yokuxhuma

• Hlela / Khansela / Buka Imisebenzi: Phatha imisebenzi ye-AI ephindaphindayo

Ubumfihlo Kuqala
U-Kai ubeka ubumfihlo bakho phambili: Wonke umlando wokukhuluma ugcinwa endaweni ngokubethela okuqinile. Uma usebenzisa izinsizakalo ze-AI zasemafini, idatha yakho ithunyelwa ngqo kubahlinzeki ukuze icutshungulwe — akukho ukuqoqwa kwedatha uhlelo lokusebenza ngokwalo. Hlala ulawula ngohlelo lokusebenza lwe-AI lokukhuluma oluvulekile.
