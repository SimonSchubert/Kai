Udinga izimpendulo ze-AI ezisheshayo ngaphandle kwezinto ezingadingekile? Kai ungumngane wakho osheshayo, ovulekile, oqhutshwa amamodeli afana no-Llama ne-Gemini yakwa-Google. Ngena ezingxoxweni ezihamba kahle zokucabanga, ukubhala, ukufingqa, ukufunda, noma izingxoxo ezijwayelekile – konke ngesixhumi esilula esilayisha ngamasekhondi.

• Gemini: I-AI ethuthukile yakwa-Google enesekelo lokufaka amafayela
• xAI: Finyelela kumadivayisi e-Grok amasha ukuthola izimpendulo ezakhayo nezinokuqonda
• Groq: Bheka ukuphendula kwe-AI okusheshayo ukuthola izimpendulo ezisheshayo
• Mistral AI: Sebenzisa isigaba samahhala sokusebenzisana ne-AI okonga izindleko

Ubumfihlo Kuqala
Kai ubeka ubumfihlo bakho phambili: Wonke umlando wengxoxo ugcinwa endaweni ngokubethela okuqinile. Hlala ulawula ngohlelo lokulayisha lwengxoxo ye-AI oluvulekile.
