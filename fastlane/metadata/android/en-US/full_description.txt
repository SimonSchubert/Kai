Kai is a cross-platform open-source AI interface that connects you to powerful conversational AI models. Through a simple and intuitive chat interface, you can engage with models from Gemini, xAI, Groq, Mistral, and self-hosted Ollama. Whether you need help brainstorming, summarizing, writing, learning, or just want to have an engaging conversation, Kai provides a versatile platform.

Supported AI Services
• Ollama - Connect to your self-hosted local AI models
• Gemini - Google's powerful AI with file attachment support
• xAI - Access Grok models
• Groq - Ultra-fast AI inference
• Mistral AI - Free tier available

Key Features
• Encrypted Local Storage: Your chat history is securely stored with encryption on your device
• Text to Speech: Listen to AI responses with built-in text-to-speech output
• File Attachments: Attach files to your conversations (Gemini)
• Seamless Service Switching: Easily switch between different AI providers
• Multi-Turn Conversations: Natural flowing conversations where the AI remembers context
• Simple Chat Interface: Intuitive design for both technical and non-technical users

AI Tools (can be enabled in settings)
• Get Local Time - AI can access current date and time
• Get Location - Location-aware responses based on IP
• Send Notification - Receive push notifications from AI
• Create Calendar Event - Create events directly from chat
• Check Recent SMS - View your SMS conversations
• Send SMS - Reply to SMS through the AI

Privacy Note: Kai stores your chat history locally with encryption. When using cloud-based AI services, your inputs are sent to the respective providers for processing. No data is collected by the app itself.
